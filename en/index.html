Introduction

This is a guide on how to read this material, including introductions to the characters and the authors.

  • 01

    Copyright Basics

    This section explains the purpose of the Copyright Act, emphasizing that the the Act is not written solely to protect rights.
  • 02

    Copyright in Classes

    This section explains the legal rules regarding the use of third-party copyrighted works in classes, including the content and definitions of terms, as well as the process for handling copyrighted works in classes.
  • 03

    Q&A on the Use of Copyrighted Works in the Classroom

    This section provides explanations in a Q&A format about the requirements for dealing with third-party copyrighted works in classes.
  • 04

    How to Obtain Permission

    When a copyrighted work cannot be freely used due to the protection period or usage conditions/terms of use, or if rights limitations cannot be applied, the permission of the copyright owner is required.
  • 05

    Quiz

    Based on what has been/you have learned, you are encouraged to try the 20-question copyright quiz.
